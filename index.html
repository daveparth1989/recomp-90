The Single Habit

01
// The one thing that makes or breaks your results
Hit your protein
every single day.

Target: 180–200 g protein/day (≈2.0–2.2 g/kg bodyweight). In a calorie deficit, protein is what prevents your 7 kg drop from being half-muscle. It's also the most satiating macro — so you will spontaneously eat less junk when you hit it early in the day.

Not training, not cardio, not supplements. If you only get one thing right for 90 days, make it this. Weigh your protein sources. Front-load protein at breakfast (40g+). The scale can fluctuate, sleep can wobble, cardio can be missed — protein is non-negotiable.

Progression Model

RULE 01
Double Progression
Add reps until you hit the top of the rep range for ALL prescribed sets. Then add weight the next session and reset to the bottom of the range.
RULE 02
Smallest Useful Jumps
Compounds: +2.5 kg when ready. Upper-body isolations: +1.25 kg (or +1 rep twice). Consistency beats jumps.
RULE 03
RPE Governs Volume
If your top set is RPE 9+ with form breaking, don't add. If it's RPE 6, add weight, not reps. Autoregulate.
RULE 04
Deload Protocol
Weeks 4 & 8: use ~60% of last week's working weights, halve the sets. Same exercises, same movement grooves — just less.
RULE 05
Never Skip The Main Lift
If time-crunched, drop accessories. The first compound of each session is the non-negotiable. Skipping it = skipping the stimulus.
RULE 06
Log Everything
If it's not logged, it didn't happen. The tracker below is the single source of truth for what you lifted last time — beat it.

Nutrition Framework

Based on your stats (92kg, 23% BF, lightly-to-moderately active 5 days/week), your maintenance sits around 2,700 kcal. The program runs on a modest ~450 kcal deficit — aggressive enough for ~0.6 kg/week fat loss, gentle enough to preserve strength and training output.

Practical execution rules
  • ▸ Weigh in daily, average the weekly numbers on Sunday. Day-to-day weight is noise.
  • ▸ Sleep 7+ hours. <6 hrs doubles hunger hormones and halves fat loss. This is not optional in a cut.
  • ▸ 10,000 steps/day minimum — this is the biggest lever outside lifting.
  • ▸ Two "refeed" days/month at maintenance calories (higher carbs) to reset leptin.
  • ▸ Hydrate: 3.5 L water/day. Thirst is often read as hunger.
  • ▸ Alcohol: ≤1 session/week, ≤3 drinks. It blunts protein synthesis for 24+ hours.
  • ▸ Creatine monohydrate: 5g/day. The single highest-evidence supplement for recomps.
